Dushan's Zlatnik - Whitepaper

The Digital Covenant of Our Existence

Introduction

Dushan's Zlatnik is a symbolic digital token inspired by the golden currency of Serbian Emperor Dushan the Mighty. It represents the digital covenant of our existence, dedicated to the preservation and restoration of Serbian holy sites. With a fixed total supply of 11,000,000 tokens, each token symbolically honors every Serb worldwide.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to support the preservation and restoration of Serbian holy sites and cultural heritage in the digital age. The vision is to create a symbol that holds spiritual and cultural value, serving as a lasting reminder of our roots.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1 – Project Launch - Smart contract deployment, website launch, and initial distribution of Dushan's Zlatnik tokens. The goal of this phase is to create a symbolic digital covenant without any profit or speculative intent.
  • Phase 2 – NFT and Fundraising - Launching an NFT collection inspired by Serbian holy sites. All proceeds from this phase will be dedicated to funds for restoration and preservation of monasteries and cultural-spiritual heritage.
  • Phase 3 – Support and Distribution - Donations from the fund will go directly to restoration projects. A transparent map of funds and activities will be established, along with ongoing support for new initiatives.
