Frequently Asked Questions about Dušan's Zlatnik

  1. What is Dušan's Zlatnik?

    Dušan's Zlatnik is a Serbian crypto token inspired by the history and legacy of Emperor Dušan. It is designed as a digital symbol of national pride and as a means to fund cultural and historical projects, including digitization and preservation of Serbian monasteries.

  2. What is the goal of the project?

    The goal is to build a community that uses blockchain technology to promote and preserve Serbian culture, history, and spiritual heritage. The third phase focuses on creating an NFT collection of Serbian monasteries.

  3. On which network is the token issued?

    Dušan's Zlatnik is issued on the BNB Smart Chain network (BEP-20 standard), enabling low transaction fees and fast transfers.

  4. When does the presale - Phase 1 start and end?

    The presale starts on August 1, 2025. It automatically ends when the tokens allocated for this phase (10% of the total supply) are sold out. The end of the presale and transition to the next phase will be announced on official channels.

  5. How can I buy tokens in the presale - Phase 1?
    • Install MetaMask (mobile app or browser extension).
    • Add the BNB Smart Chain network.
    • On the website, click the "Buy Zlatnik" button, accept terms, and enter the amount. Minimum purchase is 1 token, maximum is 110,000 (1% of total supply) to ensure fair distribution.
    • Send BNB through your wallet (rate: 1 Dušan's Zlatnik = 1 RSD ≈ 0.00001316 BNB) to the address 0xF173D56F1893aE48A42566EA4f56062e48682F67.
    • You will automatically receive tokens in your wallet.
  6. Where does the presale money go?

    80% of the collected funds will be used to secure initial liquidity for listing the token on exchanges (Phase 2).
    20% will be allocated for the first donation in line with the project mission.

  7. Is there a whitelist or entry restriction?

    No. The sale is open to everyone who meets the purchase conditions and can technically complete the transaction.

  8. What do I get when I buy tokens?

    You receive ZLATNIK tokens in your wallet, which can be used in future project phases.
    Tokens also serve as a ticket to participate in public auctions of Serbian monastery NFTs (Phase 3), as well as potential partner applications.

  9. What is Phase 3 – NFT Monasteries?

    Phase 3 involves issuing unique NFTs representing Serbian monasteries with historical descriptions, photos, and cultural significance. All proceeds from public auctions will go towards restoration and preservation of cultural heritage.

  10. Which monasteries will be included in the NFT collection?

    The first will include monasteries from Fruška Gora—16 in total—some of which are forgotten, less visited, and in poor condition, as well as many others from Serbia and the wider Serbian spiritual heritage.

  11. How does the project protect against pump-and-dump schemes, rug pulls, and similar abuses?

    Dušan's Zlatnik project has a clear and transparent plan for fund usage. The maximum token amount per buyer is limited to 1% of total supply, preventing concentration of tokens in few hands. Funds raised in presale have a precise purpose, and liquidity added to exchanges will be locked for at least 12 months, protecting against rug pulls and other abuses. All activities and transactions are publicly accessible on BscScan, ensuring full transparency.
