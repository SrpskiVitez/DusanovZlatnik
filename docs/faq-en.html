Frequently Asked Questions about Dušanov Zlatnik

1. What is Dušanov Zlatnik?
Dušanov Zlatnik is a Serbian crypto token inspired by the history and legacy of Emperor Dušan. It serves as a digital symbol of national pride and as a means to fund cultural and historical projects, including the digitization and preservation of Serbian monasteries.
2. What is the goal of the project?
The goal is to build a community that uses blockchain technology to promote and preserve Serbian culture, history, and spiritual heritage. In the third phase, special focus will be on creating an NFT collection of Serbian monasteries.
3. On which network is the token issued?
Dušanov Zlatnik is issued on the BNB Smart Chain network (BEP-20 standard), enabling low transaction fees and fast transfers.
4. When does the presale - Phase 1 start and end?
The presale starts on August 1, 2025. It ends automatically when the tokens allocated for this phase (10% of total supply) are sold out. The end of presale and transition to the next phase will be announced on official channels.
5. How can I buy tokens in the presale - Phase 1?
  1. Install MetaMask (mobile app or browser extension).
  2. Add the BNB Smart Chain network.
  3. On the website, click the “Buy Zlatnik” button, accept the terms, and enter the amount. Minimum purchase is 1 token, maximum 110,000 tokens (1% of total supply), to ensure fair distribution.
  4. Send BNB through your wallet (exchange rate: 1 Dušanov Zlatnik = 1 RSD ≈ 0.00001316 BNB) to the address 0xF173D56F1893aE48A42566EA4f56062e48682F67.
  5. You will automatically receive tokens in your wallet.
6. Where does the money from the token presale go?
80% of the raised funds will be used to provide initial liquidity to enable token listing on exchanges (Phase 2).
20% of the funds will be used to provide the first donation in accordance with the project mission.
7. Is there a whitelist or entry limitation?
No. The sale is open to everyone who respects the purchase conditions and can technically perform the transaction.
8. What do I get when I buy tokens?
You receive ZLATNIK tokens in your wallet, which you can use in future project phases.
The tokens also serve as a ticket to participate in public auctions for the sale of NFTs of Serbian monasteries (Phase 3), as well as potential partner applications.
9. What is Phase 3 – NFT Monasteries?
Phase 3 involves issuing unique NFTs representing Serbian monasteries with historical descriptions, photos, and cultural significance. All proceeds from the public auction sales will be directed towards the restoration and protection of cultural heritage.
10. Which monasteries will be included in the NFT collection?
Among the first will be monasteries from Fruška Gora, precisely 16 of them, some of which are neglected, less visited, and in poor condition, as well as many others from Serbia and the wider Serbian spiritual heritage.
11. How does the project protect against pump and dump schemes, rug pulls, and similar abuses?
The Dušanov Zlatnik project has a clear and transparent plan for using funds. The maximum token amount per buyer is limited to 1% of the total supply, preventing token concentration in the hands of a few individuals. The funds collected in the presale have a precisely defined purpose, and the liquidity added to the exchange will be locked for at least 12 months, protecting against rug pull scams and other abuses. All activities and transactions are publicly available on BscScan, ensuring full transparency.
